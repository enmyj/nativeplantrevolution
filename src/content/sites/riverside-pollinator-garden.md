This garden was established in late 2024 in the front of the Willows Nursery school. The planting area used to have a tree, but after the tree died, the area  demonstration garden and vital habitat corridor for urban pollinators.

### Grasses & Sedges
- **Purple Needle Grass** (*Stipa pulchra*) - Native bunch grass whos deep roots aerate soil and store carbon
- **Common Rush** (*Juncus patens*) - Thrives in the moist soil

## Impact

In its first season, we documented visits from native bees, hummingbirds, and arboreal salamanders.
